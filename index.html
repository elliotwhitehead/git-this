Git - A Brief History

Started in April 2005 by Linus Torvalds while building the Linux kernel.

There weren't any free version control tools which:

  • were distributed
  • worked fast
  • guaranteed consistency
"I decided I can write something better than anything out there in two weeks. And I was right." - Linus Torvalds
